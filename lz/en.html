Creator Collaboration Brief

No-Hole Magnetic Band, Instant Summer Style Upgrade

Show how one magnetic two-tone band makes daily wear easier, softer, and gives an old watch a fresher summer look.

Key Selling Points

No-Hole Magnetic DesignNo lining up tiny holes or buckle pins. Just place it on, then adjust the fit by hand.
Soft Wrist FitFlexible silicone feels skin-friendly for daily wear, commuting, and workouts.
Secondary Benefits
Two-Tone Visual PopThe inner and outer color contrast adds depth, making summer outfits and outing scenes more eye-catching.
Litchi Texture DetailThe surface texture adds close-up detail so product shots do not feel flat.

How to film the first 3 seconds

Lead with a visual problem and solve it instantly with the magnetic fit-and-adjust moment.

1. Compare the Pin-Hole BandFilm fingers trying to line up tiny holes, missing the fit, or adjusting too many times. Text: "Stop fighting with those tiny holes."
2. Show the Fast FitWrap it around the wrist, let the magnets connect, and adjust quickly. Keep the action fast and avoid listing specs first.
3. Prove the MagnetShow the band holding and wrapping with magnetic tension.

Content Production Sequence

Make it feel like a creator execution checklist: problem, proof, comfort, outfit, trust, conversion.

Opening Hook

CoreStart with the hassle of lining up tiny holes or getting the tightness wrong. Use: "Stop fighting with those tiny holes."

Wear Demo

CoreComplete the wrap, magnetic fit, and quick adjustment on camera. Focus on speed, not long specs.

Comfort Proof

CoreTouch the surface, bend it gently, and show that it feels soft, light, and easy for daily wear.

Style Upgrade

CorePair it with summer clothes, sporty looks, or an out-the-door scene to make the color feel fresh.

Trust Reminder

CoreSay this is a compatible watch band/strap, not the whole watch. Remind viewers to confirm size and color.

Conversion

Core"Which color would you pick for summer? Tap the product link and check your size before ordering."

Restricted Wording

You can say

  • compatible with Apple Watch
  • smart watch case

Do not say

  • This is an Apple watch case

Product-specific notes

Keep the main content brand-light. Use "watch band" or "strap" often. Do not imply the watch/device is included, do not claim official Apple affiliation, and avoid saying it can never come off.

Creator Deliverable Checklist

Film a vertical 8-26 second demo with the magnetic fit shown in the first three seconds. End with: "If your current one still feels boring, this is such an easy upgrade. Tap the product link and choose your color."
